blog NO.2

Identifies the group of people targeted in the video

The group of people targeted in the video is the fans of computer games or video games. They spend a lot of time on playing the games and ignore the normal socialization in real world, and they may think the socialization in the game is the same with the real life socialization.

Explains the social issue at hand

The social issue in the video talks about how the “World Of Warcraft” changes these players’ life. The games influence more and more people to get stick in the website and games. They play games, do not hang out and get lazy in the life due to playing game so much.

Identifies and critiques the video's creators' view on that social issue

The video’s creator may want people pay more attention on the normal socialization and decrease the time on the game. The video’s creator may also expect the game companies do some efforts on making people life with normal socialization, or decreasing the bad influences on the player, and pay more attention on their healthy.
