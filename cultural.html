Executive Summary

The artifact I write about is the popular TV show called “Breaking Bad” which talks about a nice chemist become drug cooker and seller. It is a TV shows talk about crime. Within this show, I saw a nice people work hard but still loss his “American Dream,” then he changed to a darkness side and do crime. These causes me thinking about how “American Dream” change people and what the “American Dream” changed in these years? Does it all good? What can correct the “American Dream” and more positive around it? I use story of “Breaking Bad” as the example to induce the review of “American Dream” and discussion.
