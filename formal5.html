Technology Section

The technology has been improved a lot in these years. For example, 10 years before, the cellphone was just a tool to phone and message the people. How about now? Nowadays, we prefer call the cellphone more as the smart phone, because it is really smart than before. We can send E-mail, video with others, surfer the internet and play games on smart phone. The companies of smartphone do not just focus on the basic function, phone and message, but more about the system, outlook and hardware design. The smartphone get megapixel camera, the higher screen display resolution and dual-core even quad-core CPU (Central Processing Unit) on the smart phone. The outlook of phones gets revolution, too. Some companies use the glass material as outlook; some use the metal as the outlook of the phones and some use the Polycarbonate Plastic as the outlook design. Different materials bring different feeling to the customers and offer more options to the public. These improvements bring the better quality of the life and strength the useful of the phone.

When the equipment gets improved faster, the internet also gets improved upon with the equipment. Several years ago, most mobile internet was 2G pattern, a kind of old and slow digit signal. This was one reason causes the cellphones work with little functions. However, after the 3G internet pattern designed out, the more function was available for the cellphones because when the speed of connection get faster, the user experience can get better with high connection speed. And now, the mobile internet gets grown into 4G period. The progress of the internet and electrical equipment bring more confident to the people to design the more concept and products, such as smart TV, smart glasses, and smart watch. Within the “smart” period, those design and equipment can bring more convenience and efficiency to the public and daily life.

The technology does not only bring the new and cool stuffs and concept, and it also effect the society a lot. The technology brings economic profits to the individual and the public; also improve the life quality. Nowadays, we can sit at home but know the news around world, due to the internet. However, speed of news is limited by the hard carry of computer. We have to go office or home; then open the computer to get information long ago. With fashion of the smart phone and the WIFI solve the problems, people is capable to view news on the subway, on the street, and even on the way to the home or office. And the fashion of the Twitter and Facebook accelerate the information become open and transparent; also offer people more opportunities to meet more friends. The mobile equipment, such as smartphone and Ipad, and the better mobile internet also bring a lot economic profits to the society. With the popular of these, customers can buy stuffs online instead of going to store more conveniently. What is more is that most time the stuffs online is cheap than the store due to the low cost, and it can save the money for the customers. As well as the customers save money by E-business, there are more and more people do the E-business as individual or as companies. These changes can bring more job opportunities for the society. The grown and grown technology companies can also bring job opportunities to the public. Just like a business chain, the more equipment needed, the more people the factory hires for achieve the market requirement. Those are the appearance of the social responsibility the technology brought to the public.
