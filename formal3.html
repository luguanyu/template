Background Information

As a common concept, the engineering is a major which is more practical in life compared to other knowledge. When mention the computer engineering, we have to divide it into two parts, computer and engineering. The “computer” means the computer science, which focus more on the software, and the “engineering” means the electrical engineering, which focus on the hardware. In one sentence, it means the major “computer engineering” covers hardware design and software design. More actually definition is that the “computer engineering” is a combined major with “computer science” and “electrical engineering.” The main work for the computer engineer is for hardware and software design and some test for the product design. Like most engineer do, the computer engineers require accurately and serious attitude in the work, sophisticated skill, and the great teamwork is also necessary. Computer Engineering is kind of new major for university and career. When employer realized they need some professional people skillful in both software and hard ware design, the computer engineering came out as a new major to fill market necessary. Nowadays, the computer engineering plays a significant role in the technology companies, and their products are also used in multiple careers. No matter the smartphone or some new tech product, they can not be designed and produced without the knowledge of computer engineers. When I studied in high school, I felt interested in electrical product. As the interesting to the electrical product, I studied the C++ language, a kind of computer system language, and I also read news about the career. When the new technology comes out? What is it? How is the future of the new technology? The more I read, the more I learn about this career. People always say that the hobby is the best teacher to the students. Thus, when I decided to study in America, I choose the Computer Engineering as my major without hesitation. That is the story about me and my major.
